Calculating the Daily Gas Production

\( G = VS \times G_y \)

\( m^3 / day = kg \times m^3 / (d \times kg) \)

where, \( G \) is amount of Biogas Generated, \( VS \) is Volatile Solids Content and, \( G_y \) is Specific Gas Yield.

\[ VS \] \[ G_y \] \[ G \]

\( G_p = G \div V_d \)

\( m^3 / day \div m^3 \)

where, \( G_p \) is Specific Gas Production, \( G \) is amount of Biogas generated and, \( V_d \) is Digester Volume.

\[ G \] \[ V_d \] \[ G_p \]
